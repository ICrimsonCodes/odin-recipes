Pancakes

Pancakes are a classic breakfast dish made from a simple batter of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder. They can be served with a variety of toppings such as syrup, fruit, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, eggs, milk, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, sugar, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface of the pancake and the edges start to dry out, about 2 minutes.
  6. Flip the pancake and cook until the other side is golden brown, about 1 minute more.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter.
