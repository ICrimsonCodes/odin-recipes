Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink, about 8 minutes.
  4. Drain excess fat from skillet.
  5. Add ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and marinara sauce to skillet. Stir to combine.
  6. Add egg and oregano to skillet. Stir to combine.
  7. Spoon mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Bake for 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and bubbly.
  9. Remove from oven and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
